About Me

I'm a cybersecurity and compliance professional based in Omaha, NE — currently finishing a B.S. in Applied Computing with double minors in Cybersecurity and Management Information Systems at the University of Nebraska at Omaha (GPA 3.7, Chancellor's List).

I hold both CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701, passed August 2025) and CompTIA A+ certifications, and I work daily in security operations, log analysis with Splunk, API security, and compliance frameworks like HIPAA and SOC 2.

Through my independent consulting practice on Upwork, I've supported small businesses and healthcare-adjacent startups with risk registers, vendor security reviews, HIPAA and SOC 2 readiness, and cybersecurity training content. I also rewrote the documentation for a major cybersecurity platform (under NDA) and have served as a SOC analyst using tools like Kaseya, RocketCyber, and Datto.

Day-to-day at Toast, I perform advanced log analysis in Splunk, validate OAuth/token-based authentication flows via Postman, triage API integration failures, and support security escalations — including one incident I uncovered that led to fundamental security controls being added for a third-party vendor.

Outside of work, I run a multi-host homelab (Kali, Ubuntu Server, Windows Server) for hands-on security practice, solve CTF challenges daily on Hack The Box and TryHackMe, and experiment with local AI models for log parsing and automation. I'm also the cofounder and president of the UNO Mindfulness Club — 4+ years of building community and leading peer mentorship programs.
